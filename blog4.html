Core PHP Basics

Published on Nov 2024 | by Puneet Tomar

Before Laravel, I worked extensively with Core PHP. Understanding how PHP works without frameworks helped me become a stronger developer.

What I Learned from Core PHP

  • Manual handling of sessions, cookies, and authentication
  • Writing raw SQL queries for CRUD operations
  • Building reusable functions and classes
  • Understanding server requests and responses

This foundation made it easier to transition into Laravel and understand why frameworks automate many tasks.

If you’re new, I recommend building at least 2–3 apps in Core PHP before jumping to frameworks.
