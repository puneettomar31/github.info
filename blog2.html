Optimizing WordPress for Speed

Published on Jan 2025 | by Puneet Tomar

During my internship as a WordPress developer, one of my main tasks was to optimize client websites for speed and SEO. Many websites were heavy due to plugins and unoptimized themes.

My Go-To Optimization Steps

  • Removing unnecessary plugins and scripts
  • Using lightweight, custom themes
  • Optimizing images with compression tools
  • Adding caching (e.g., WP Super Cache, LiteSpeed)
  • Configuring CDN for global delivery

As a result, I improved performance scores and reduced load times from 6+ seconds to under 2 seconds. This directly improved client SEO rankings.

Lesson learned: Speed matters as much as design.
